Handcrafted Gifts Since 2019

Aesthetic Gift By Khosi Mhayise creates elegant, handcrafted gift hampers designed to celebrate life’s special moments. Every hamper is curated with premium locally-sourced products — from artisanal treats to fragrant candles and luxury stationery.

We specialise in birthdays, weddings, baby showers, anniversary gifts, and corporate hampers. Our mission is to make gifting meaningful, effortless, and beautiful: choose your theme, add a personalised note, and we’ll handle the rest.
